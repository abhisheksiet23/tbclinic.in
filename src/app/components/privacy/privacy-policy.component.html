Privacy Policy — MEDCROSS by TB Clinic

Operated by UshaPrakash Health Care Private Limited

Your privacy and trust matter deeply to us. This policy explains, in clear language, how we collect and use your information so that we can deliver safe, ethical and effective healthcare services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Profile Information — Name, Age, Contact Number, Email
  • Health Records — Symptoms, Test Reports, Treatment Notes
  • App & Device Usage — App navigation, crash logs, device type

2. Why Your Data Is Used

We use your information exclusively for healthcare purposes:

  • Track TB and diabetes treatment progress
  • Help doctors make informed clinical decisions
  • Manage appointments and patient records
  • Send medication reminders and alerts
  • Improve app performance and prevent fraud
  • Meet public health compliance and reporting laws

We never sell your personal data. Limited data may be shared with partner pharmacies and labs to process prescriptions and tests.

3. When Your Data May Be Shared

Shared With Purpose
Our Doctors & Counselors To provide medical treatment
Cloud, SMS & Analytics Services To operate and secure the app
Partner Pharmacies & Labs To deliver medicines and conduct tests
Government Agencies (only if required) Legal compliance with health laws

All partners follow strict confidentiality and data protection standards.

4. Your Rights

  • Request a copy of your health data
  • Request corrections or updates

We respond within 15 working days.

5. Data Security

  • End-to-End Encryption in transfer & storage
  • Access limited to authorized medical staff
